Lesson 1 — Hangul Basics: Consonants & Vowels
Goal: Learn the Korean alphabet (Hangul).
Topics:

Basic consonants: ㄱ ㄴ ㄷ ㄹ ㅁ ㅅ ㅇ ㅎ

Basic vowels: ㅏ ㅓ ㅗ ㅜ ㅡ ㅣ

Syllable block structure (CV, CVC)

Vocabulary:

None (focus on alphabet)

Sentence Practice:

Reading simple syllables: 가, 너, 다, 로, 미, 사

Lesson 2 — Hangul: Combined Vowels & Batchim
Goal: Complete Hangul & reading practice.
Topics:

Complex vowels: ㅐ ㅒ ㅔ ㅖ ㅘ ㅙ ㅚ ㅝ ㅞ ㅟ ㅢ

Batchim (final consonants): ㄱ, ㄴ, ㄹ, ㅁ

Reading real words

Vocabulary:

밥 (rice)

물 (water)

집 (house)

삶 (life)

Sentences:

밥 먹어요 (I eat rice)

Lesson 3 — Introductions & Polite Form
Goal: Polite beginner sentences for greeting people.
Topics:

Polite ending: -요

Subject markers: 는 / 은

Basic greetings

Vocabulary:

안녕하세요 — hello

저는 — I

이름 — name

사람 — person

Sentences:

안녕하세요

저는 David이에요

저는 미국사람이에요 (I am American)

Lesson 4 — Essential Verbs & Present Tense
Goal: Learn useful verbs in present polite tense.
Topics:

Present tense: -아요 / -어요 / -해요

Basic verbs

Vocabulary:

먹다 — to eat

가다 — to go

오다 — to come

하다 — to do

Sentences:

저는 밥을 먹어요

저는 학교에 가요

Lesson 5 — Locations & Going Places
Goal: Navigating simple locations.
Topics:

Location particle: 에 (to/at)

Basic places

Basic direction terms

Vocabulary:

학교 — school

식당 — restaurant

집 — home

여기 — here

저기 — there

Sentences:

저는 식당에 가요 (I go to the restaurant)

여기에 있어요 (It’s here)

Lesson 6 — Food & Ordering
Goal: Ordering food at restaurants and cafes.
Topics:

주세요 (please give me)

Numbers for counting things (1–4 native: 한, 두, 세, 네)

Food vocabulary

Vocabulary:

비빔밥 — bibimbap

물 — water

커피 — coffee

하나 — one

둘 — two

Sentences:

물 하나 주세요

비빔밥 맛있어요 (Bibimbap is delicious)

Lesson 7 — Describing Things (Adjectives)
Goal: Describe taste, weather, people.
Topics:

Adjectives in present form

Basic descriptive adjectives

Vocabulary:

맵다 — spicy

달다 — sweet

좋다 — good

작다 — small

크다 — big

Sentences:

이거 매워요 (This is spicy)

Lesson 8 — Time, Days, & Simple Scheduling
Goal: Tell time and talk about daily plans.
Topics:

Hours/minutes

Days of the week

Verb + 고 싶어요 (want to...)

Vocabulary:

오늘 — today

내일 — tomorrow

시간 — time

보고 싶어요 — want to see/watch

하고 싶어요 — want to do

Sentences:

내일 만나요 (See you tomorrow)

영화 보고 싶어요 (I want to watch a movie)

Lesson 9 — Directions & Transportation
Goal: Navigating cities while traveling.
Topics:

Transportation verbs

Right/left/straight

Vocabulary:

어디 — where

오른쪽 — right

왼쪽 — left

지하철 — subway

버스 — bus

Sentences:
